cybersecurity

Capacity of protecting your personal data from unauthorized aces or criminal activity. In most cases through the use of specialized software, that protects your devices from potential digital ransom

white hackers

Ethical users that uses its hacking skills for identifying vulnerabilities in hardware, software and networks , in seek of creating more secure systems for protecting all users. Most of them tend to be black hackers, but usually they are hired by companies for protecting their servers, web pages, among other platforms.

VPNS

Establishes a digital connection between your computer and a remote server owned by a VPN provider, creating a point-to-point tunnel that encrypts your personal data, masks your IP address, and lets you sidestep website blocks and firewalls on the internet.

cyberciminals, Lockbit

LockBit operates as a ransomware-as-a-service operation, with a core handful of members creating its malware and running its website and infrastructure. This core group licenses its code to “affiliates,” who launch attacks against companies, steal their data, and try to extort money from them.
